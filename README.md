# ParkEasy

## Community-Based Real-Time Parking Platform

ParkEasy is a community-based platform designed to help drivers find available parking spaces in real time.

The platform connects drivers who are about to leave a parking space with drivers who are currently searching for parking in the same area.

> **Stop searching, start parking.**

---

## About the Project

Finding parking in crowded urban areas is often frustrating, time-consuming, and unpredictable.

Drivers may spend a long time driving around without knowing where or when a parking space will become available. This can lead to delays, fuel waste, traffic congestion, and air pollution.

ParkEasy provides a smarter solution by allowing drivers to share parking availability with other users in real time.

When a driver is about to leave a parking space, they can report it through the application. Nearby drivers can then view the location on the map and navigate toward it.

---

## Main Goal

The main goal of ParkEasy is to transform parking search from a long and uncertain process into a simple, fast, and community-driven experience.

The system aims to:

- Reduce parking search time
- Reduce driver frustration
- Reduce unnecessary traffic
- Reduce fuel consumption
- Improve the urban parking experience
- Build an active and reliable driver community

## How It Works

### Driver Leaving a Parking Space

1. The driver opens the application.
2. The driver selects **“I’m Leaving.”**
3. The system receives the current GPS location.
4. A temporary parking report is created.
5. Nearby drivers can view the parking location.

### Driver Searching for Parking

1. The driver opens the application.
2. Nearby parking reports are displayed on the map.
3. The driver selects a relevant parking space.
4. The application opens navigation to the selected location.

---

## Community Model

ParkEasy is based on a Peer-to-Peer community model.

The information is generated by the users themselves rather than relying only on sensors or municipal systems.

Users are encouraged to submit reliable reports through:

- Community points
- Reliability ratings
- Rewards
- ParkCoins
- Achievements
- Future leaderboards

As the number of users and reports increases, the platform becomes more useful and accurate for the entire community.

---

## Technical Concepts

### Geofencing

Geofencing is used to verify that the reporting user is physically located near the parking space.

This helps reduce false or inaccurate parking reports.

### Spatial Queries

Spatial Queries are used to locate parking reports according to geographic distance.

The system displays only parking spaces that are close and relevant to the searching driver.

### Concurrency Control

Concurrency Control prevents multiple users from relying on or selecting the same parking report at the same time.

The system updates the parking status and handles simultaneous actions correctly.
